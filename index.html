Osservatorio didattica educazione 2013/02

Basic bookbinding manual focused on the use of recycled materials and makeshift tools. The publication has been written and designed after a series of workshops held towards the end of 2011 in Bari. Afterwards, we replicated the workshop around Italy, and actively improve the course and promote it in other venues.
